Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Volunteer Connect collects, uses, and protects the personal information you provide to us.

Information We Collect

  • Personal details (e.g., name, email address) provided during sign-up or registration.
  • Usage data, such as pages visited and actions performed on the site.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide you with updates on volunteer opportunities and events.
  • To respond to inquiries and support requests.
  • To improve our website and services.

Information Sharing

Volunteer Connect does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as required by law or to provide services you have requested.

Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
